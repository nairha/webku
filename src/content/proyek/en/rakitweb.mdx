### Here are the Complete Details of RakitWeb's Services:

We are not tied to just one template or one CMS. The RakitWeb developer team is skilled at building various types of websites, including:
- **Company Profile Websites** & Landing Pages (institutional branding).
- **E-Commerce Websites** (Online Stores, Marketplaces, catalog systems).

- **Community Websites** & Discussion Forums.

#### 2. Hosting & Domain Services (Registration and Management)
RakitWeb provides powerful hosting infrastructure with high specifications (using SSD NVMe, LiteSpeed, and DDoS protection). Our domain services include:
- Searching for and registering various extensions (.com, .id, .net, .org, etc.).
- DNS management and domain migration between providers.
- **Indonesian Hosting** with servers located in Jakarta for maximum access speed, as well as **Overseas Hosting** (Singapore/USA) for global market targets.

This is a plus point that few other service providers have. We understand that many institutions or startups already have infrastructure on **Vercel, Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud Platform (GCP), or DigitalOcean**.

RakitWeb is ready to be your **"Technical Right Hand"** without forcing you to switch providers. We provide services for:
- **Managed Maintenance**: 24/7 server monitoring, routine security updates, and bug patches on your application code.
- **Performance Optimization**: Configuring database settings, caching, and CDN so your website on AWS/Vercel remains fast.
- **Backup & Disaster Recovery**: Scheduling automatic backups and ensuring your data is safe.
- **DevOps Support**: Managing deployment pipelines, staging/production environments, and automatic scaling during traffic spikes.

In essence, you still own your account on Vercel/AWS, but *we handle the work*. You just focus on your business, we take care of the technicalities.
