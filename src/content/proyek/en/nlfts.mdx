### What is NLFTs?

**NLFTs** is an Indonesian developer community focused on **Developer Experience (Dx)**, development of **tools & libraries**, as well as high‑level technical discussions among developers. This community is designed as an intensive collaboration space that operates **24 hours non‑stop**, exclusively for developers in Indonesia.

NLFTs is not just a chat group — it is a developer ecosystem that has its own **standards**, its own **license**, and its own **governance system** known as **Monocracy**.

## Vision & Mission

### Vision

> **To build a modern, independent, and globally competitive Indonesian developer community — with a focus on developing a local technology ecosystem that adapts modern standards.**

NLFTs envisions Indonesia where developers are not just *consumers* of foreign technology, but also *creators* of tools, libraries, and platforms used by other developers — in Indonesia and worldwide.

### Mission

1. **Develop a Local Dx Ecosystem** — Build Developer Tools and Libraries born from Indonesia, for Indonesia, and ready to go global.
2. **Elevate Nuxt.js in Indonesia** — Make Nuxt the flagship framework and change the perception that Nuxt is "overlooked" in the Indonesian developer ecosystem.
3. **Set Our Own Standards** — Through the FTs Standard and FTs License, NLFTs establishes specific quality and rules for the internal ecosystem.
4. **Create Quality 24/7 Discussions** — Provide a technical discussion space that never sleeps, where every question and opinion is valued.
5. **Educate Through Technology Sessions** — Hold monthly sessions to evaluate new technologies: which are worth adopting, which should be abandoned.
6. **Build Technological Independence** — Reduce dependence on old, inefficient technologies and encourage adoption of modern ones.
7. **Maintain Exclusivity Without Elitism** — Open to all developers who meet the fundamental requirements, regardless of background.

## Entry Paths

#### 1. Discord (Open)

- **Access:** All developers who meet the qualifications
- **Restriction:** Must be at least 17 years old (per Discord policy)
- **Exclusivity level:** **Low** — the widest entry point
- **How to join:** Invite link → Age verification → Enter

#### 2. WhatsApp Group (Open with Conditions)

- **Access:** All developers who meet the qualifications
- **Restriction:** No age limit other than general qualifications
- **Exclusivity level:** **Medium** — has a clean‑up mechanism
- **How to join:** Referral link → Qualification verification → Enter

- **Access:** Only through selection based on real contributions
- **Restriction:** Must have a track record of contributions to NLFTs
- **Exclusivity level:** **High** — the most valuable room
- **Entry criteria:**
  - Actively submit Pull Requests
  - Actively fix issues
  - Participate in high‑value discussions
  - Recognised by the Core Team or Leaders
- **Benefits:**
  - Access to exclusive channels
  - Greater voice in technical discussions
  - Priority in events and programmes
  - Highest reputation in the NLFTs ecosystem

A member may be removed from NLFTs for:

## NLFTs Standards & License

NLFTs has two internal regulatory pillars that distinguish it from any other community:

### FTs Standard

**FTs Standard** is the quality standard set by NLFTs for:

- **Code:** Writing style, structure, naming conventions, architecture
- **Tools & Libraries:** Minimum quality to be considered part of the NLFTs ecosystem
- **Documentation:** Format, depth, language
- **Contributions:** Review process, PRs, issue tracking
- **Events:** Organisation standards, materials, speaker quality

The FTs Standard is **evolving** — updated through monthly sessions based on technological developments.

### FTs License

**FTs License** is the license applied to all outputs born from the NLFTs ecosystem, including:

- Tools & Libraries developed under NLFTs
- Official documentation
- Written standards and policies
- Event and seminar materials

> Full details of the FTs Standard and FTs License are available in separate documents.

## Technology Focus

### Main Priorities

NLFTs currently has **3 main technology pillars** that are the focus of development and promotion:

#### 1. Nuxt.js (Focus #1)

**Why Nuxt?**

- Nuxt.js is often **overlooked** in Indonesia compared to Next.js
- NLFTs is determined to **build Nuxt's reputation** in Indonesia
- Nuxt has excellent Dx, aligning with NLFTs' focus on Developer Experience
- Vision: Make Nuxt the **first choice** for Indonesian developers for serious projects

**What NLFTs does for Nuxt:**
- In‑depth discussions about Nuxt architecture
- Development of Nuxt modules and plugins
- Education through sessions and events
- Contributions to the global Nuxt ecosystem

#### 2. TypeScript + Vue.js (Focus #2)

- TypeScript as the main language standard
- Vue.js as the UI framework of choice
- The TS + Vue combination provides the best Dx for modern application development
- Encourage full TypeScript adoption (strict mode)

#### 3. Rust (Focus #3)

- Rust as the language for tooling, CLI, and performance‑critical components
- Fits NLFTs' focus on Dx Tools
- Build a solid Rust community in Indonesia
- Bridge between Rust and the JS/TS ecosystem (WASM, NAPI, etc.)

### Supported Technologies

Besides the 3 main priorities, NLFTs also supports and discusses:

| Category | Technologies |
|----------|--------------|
| **Backend** | NestJS, Hono, Bun, Deno, Fastify, tRPC |
| **Frontend** | Vue 3, React (as alternative), Svelte, Solid |
| **Mobile** | Flutter, React Native, Tauri (desktop) |
| **Database** | PostgreSQL, Redis, MongoDB, Drizzle ORM, Prisma |
| **DevOps** | Docker, Kubernetes, GitHub Actions, Vercel, Cloudflare |
| **Languages** | TypeScript, JavaScript, Rust, Go, Python, Kotlin |
| **Tooling** | Vite, Turbo, Nx, Biome, pnpm |

### Technologies Not Supported

NLFTs explicitly **does not discuss, support, or encourage** the use of technologies considered **inefficient** or **traditional**:

| Not Supported | Reason |
|---------------|--------|
| **PHP 8 and below** | Old technology, low Dx, does not fit modern vision |
| **Ruby** | Does not fit NLFTs ecosystem focus |
| **jQuery** | Already obsolete for modern projects |
| **Plain CSS without preprocessor/framework** | Inefficient for large scale |
| **Technologies without active maintenance** | Security and compatibility risks |
| **Technologies that violate Dx principles** | Contradicts the core focus of NLFTs |

> **Note:** This list is re‑evaluated at every monthly session. Technologies may be moved from "not supported" to "supported" if there is a strong reason, and vice versa.

## Programmes & Activities

### Monthly Session

The **Session** is NLFTs' most unique activity. This is not a regular meetup — it is a **technology court**.

**Format:**

1. **Submission** — A technology, framework, tool, or practice is proposed for evaluation
2. **Discussion** — Pros and cons are debated in depth by all members
3. **Verdict** — Decision on whether the technology should be:
   - **Adopted** — Recommended for use in the NLFTs ecosystem
   - **Monitored** — No conclusion yet, further observation needed
   - **Abandoned** — Considered inefficient or irrelevant
4. **Documentation** — Session results are documented and influence the FTs Standard

**Frequency:** Once per month  
**Participants:** All members (priority vote for Contributors and Core Team)

### Events & Seminars

- Frequency: **Regular** (more than once a month)
- Format: Offline (Bandung & other cities) + Online
- Scope: Tech talks, workshops, hackathons, project showcases
- Standard: All events must meet the minimum quality of the FTs Standard

### 24‑Hour Discussion

- Takes place on **Discord** as the primary platform
- Topics: Anything relevant to development
- No "sleeping hours" — there are always members online
- High‑value discussions are archived and can be used as references

### Dx Tools & Library Development

- Collective development of tools and libraries
- Uses the FTs Standard as the quality benchmark
- Uses the FTs License as the license
- Managed through the NLFTs GitHub Organisation

## Code of Conduct

### General Principles

1. **Respect fellow developers** — No question is stupid, no answer is insulted
2. **Focus on technology** — Political, SARA, and non‑technical topics outside designated channels are strictly prohibited
3. **Real contributions** — Better to provide solutions than just criticise
4. **Maintain quality** — Every message, PR, and document reflects NLFTs
5. **Be self‑reliant** — Look for answers yourself before asking, but do not hesitate to ask if stuck
6. **Integrity** — Do not plagiarise, do not claim others' work

### Prohibited Actions

| Prohibited | Sanction |
|------------|----------|
| Spam, unauthorised promotion | Warning → Kick |
| Politics, SARA, hate speech | **Permanent kick** |
| Falsifying qualifications/skills | **Permanent kick + blacklist** |
| Distributing illegal content | **Permanent kick + report to authorities** |
| Personal attacks (not attacking arguments) | Warning → Mute → Kick |
| Leaking exclusive content to the public | **Permanent kick + blacklist** |
| Misusing the NLFTs name for personal gain | **Permanent kick + legal action** |

### Violations & Sanctions

> Severe violations (Levels 4‑6) may be applied **without going through previous levels**, at the discretion of the Leader or authorised Core Team.

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is NLFTs free?**  
A: Yes, all access to the community is free. No membership fees.

**Q: Do I have to live in Bandung?**  
A: No. NLFTs is based in Bandung but operates throughout Indonesia online.

**Q: I am still a student, can I join?**  
A: Yes, as long as you meet the qualification requirements (strong fundamentals, have used a framework, have 3+ projects) and are at least 17 years old.

**Q: Why are PHP and Ruby not supported?**  
A: Because NLFTs' vision is to build a modern and efficient ecosystem. Traditional technologies do not align with the Dx focus that is the core of the community. This decision can be reviewed at the monthly session.

### Membership

**Q: I was removed from WhatsApp due to inactivity, can I re‑join?**  
A: **No.** Removal due to inactivity is permanent. However, you can still join via Discord.

**Q: How do I become a Discord Contributor?**  
A: Consistently contribute — submit PRs, fix issues, provide quality answers in discussions. Contributor status is not something you apply for, it is **recognised**.

**Q: I am a senior developer with 10 years of experience but do not have 3 projects to show, can I join?**  
A: Projects can be in any form — open source, internal company, freelance, or even side projects. As long as they can be verified.

### Technology

**Q: Why focus on Nuxt instead of Next?**  
A: Because Nuxt has excellent Dx and is currently underrepresented in Indonesia. NLFTs wants to fill this gap, not follow the mainstream.

**Q: I am a React/Next developer, am I not welcome?**  
A: Very welcome! NLFTs' focus on Nuxt does not mean we close ourselves off from other ecosystems. Discussions about React/Next are still allowed, only the development and promotion priority is Nuxt.

**Q: Does NLFTs develop its own framework?**  
A: The current focus is more on **tools and libraries** on top of existing frameworks (especially Nuxt). However, this may change in the future through sessions.
